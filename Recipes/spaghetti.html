Filipino Spaghetti

A scrumptions Filipino rendition of an italian classic, best served with mass produced processed cheese

A staple meal of the peenoise, which contributes both to their strength and stature.

Only the most generic pasta should be used, along with canned processed sauce, and served hot.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp Salt
  • 36 oz Water

Spaghetti Sauce Ingredients

  • 1 cup banana ketchup
  • 8 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp Garlic minced
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 4 pcs hotdog sliced
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 onion diced
  • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese shredded
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Tomato paste

Recipe instructions

  1. In a large pot, pour the water in and bring to a boil
  2. Season with salt
  3. Add the Spaghetti Noodles and cook until tender (see package for cooking time) then set aside
  4. Using a separate pan, saut√© the garlic and onions
  5. Insert the round meat and let cook for 5 minutes
  6. Add the hotdogs and cook for 2 minutes
  7. Put-in the tomato sauce, banana ketchup, tomato paste, and brown sugar then simmer for 15 to 20 minutes
  8. Place the sauce on top of the cooked noodles and add some cheese
  9. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!
